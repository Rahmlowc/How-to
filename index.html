The Recipe

Ingredents:

  • 2 3/4 cup of All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 teaspoon of Baking Soad
  • 1/2 teaspoon of Salt
  • 1 1/4 cup of Margarine
  • 2 cups of White Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of Vinila Extract
  • 1/4 cup of White Sugar for decorating

Directions

Frist you need to pre heat the oven to 350 degrees F. Then You start to prep
the cookie dough. In a medium size bowl mix your dry ingredence together:
flour, baking soda, and salt. Then in a seperate bowl mix together:
margarine, 2 cups of sugar, eggs, and vinilla extract. Then slowly mix the
dry ingredence together until they are evenly mixed together.

Once the cookie dough is througholy mixed together, roll them in 1 inch
sized balls and roll the balls into the 1/4 cup of sugar, and place them on a non-greesed
pan spaced evenly out. Place them in the oven for 8-10 minutes in the preheated oven.
Let cool on the pan for 5 minutes before removing them to the wire rack to cool down.
